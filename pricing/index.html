Pricing

Workforce Comply offers a single bundled subscription that includes Louisiana CNA registry lookups and OIG Exclusion/Adverse checks.

  • Up to 75 employees — $49 / month
  • 76–200 employees — $99 / month
  • 201–400 employees — $149 / month
  • Multi-facility or corporate groups — Custom pricing

Pricing is based on workforce size and designed to support predictable HR documentation workflows.

Workforce Comply provides documentation support tools and does not determine employment eligibility or guarantee regulatory compliance.
