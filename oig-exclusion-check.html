What is an OIG Exclusion Check?

An OIG exclusion check verifies whether an employee or applicant appears on the federal exclusion list maintained by the Office of Inspector General. Healthcare providers must avoid employing excluded individuals in roles tied to federally funded programs.

Why OIG Checks Matter

Hiring or retaining an excluded individual can create major compliance risk, repayment exposure, and audit problems. OIG screening is a standard part of healthcare HR compliance and should be performed consistently for new hires and ongoing workforce reviews.

How WorkforceComply Helps

WorkforceComply helps healthcare HR teams run OIG exclusion checks, flag potential issues, and generate downloadable proof packages for records, audits, and compliance documentation.
