Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to perform Louisiana compliance checks, including names and Social Security Numbers provided by the user. We also collect basic account information such as email addresses for billing and support.

2. How We Use Data

Data is used exclusively to query the Louisiana Department of Health (LDH) and CNA registries. We do not sell, rent, or share this data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Security & Retention

To protect sensitive workforce data, WorkforceComply maintains a strict retention policy. All uploaded data and generated reports are permanently deleted from our servers after five (5) business days. Users are responsible for downloading and securing their own records within this window.

4. Security Standards

We use industry-standard encryption (SSL/HTTPS) to protect data during transmission. Access to our internal systems is restricted to authorized personnel only.
