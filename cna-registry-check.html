What is a CNA Registry Check?

A CNA registry check verifies whether a Certified Nursing Assistant is active, licensed, and eligible to work. Healthcare facilities must confirm CNA status before hiring and during employment to remain compliant with state and federal regulations.

Why CNA Verification Matters

Hiring a CNA without proper verification can lead to compliance violations, fines, and failed audits. WorkforceComply helps healthcare HR teams quickly run CNA registry checks and generate proof for personnel files.

How WorkforceComply Helps

WorkforceComply allows you to upload your employee list and run CNA registry checks alongside OIG exclusion and adverse action screenings. Each run generates downloadable proof for audit documentation.
