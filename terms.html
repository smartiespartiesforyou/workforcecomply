Terms of Service

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Scope of Service

WorkforceComply provides a point-in-time lookup service against publicly available Louisiana CNA registries and the LDH Adverse Actions list. We provide documentation support tools and do not determine employment eligibility.

2. Record Retention Policy

To ensure maximum data security, WorkforceComply does not act as a system of record. Generated PDF verification records are available for download for exactly five (5) business days. After this window, records are purged and cannot be recovered.

3. Accuracy of Data

While we pull data directly from official Louisiana state sources, WorkforceComply is not responsible for errors, omissions, or delays in the state’s primary databases (LDH or Nurse Aide Registry).

4. Professional Responsibility

Users are responsible for ensuring their facility meets all Louisiana state and federal compliance mandates. Use of this tool does not guarantee regulatory compliance.
