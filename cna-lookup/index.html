OIG Exclusion/Adverse Check

This tool supports documentation of checks against the Office of Inspector General (OIG) exclusion list for healthcare workforce compliance.

Generates time-stamped, employee-specific PDF records suitable for personnel files, clearly labeled with source and date.

How This Tool Is Used

This tool is intended to support internal HR documentation of exclusion checks.

Employers are responsible for performing required verifications and reviewing results in accordance with applicable regulations and facility policy.
